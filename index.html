We envision a world where all individuals are valued, respected, and empowered, regardless of their intersecting identities, and where systems of oppression are dismantled.

Intersectional Equality Alliance is committed to advancing gender equality and social justice by recognizing and addressing the interconnected nature of discrimination based on race, ethnicity, class, sexuality, gender identity, and other intersecting identities.
